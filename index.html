The Golem Took My Family

Your family has been kidnapped by the Golem and dragged into his lair in the swamp. To free them, you have to first kill the golem with your axe [SPACE] and second move the boxes [ARROW KEYS], in which your family is being held, to the marked spots on the ground. You can only push the boxes, not pull them.
